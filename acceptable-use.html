Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: 2026 · Muneris Management Ltd

MediaHelm Live is self-hosted software that acts as a tuner bridge for personal media servers (Plex, Jellyfin, Emby and other HDHomeRun-compatible players). This policy sets out, in plain terms, what MediaHelm Live is, what it is not, and how it must be used.

MediaHelm Live supplies no content. MediaHelm Live ships with no channels, no streams, no playlists, no media and no subscriptions. Every source is provided, owned and controlled by you, on your own hardware.

MediaHelm Live provides no content

MediaHelm Live does not supply, host, sell, resell, stream, aggregate, index, scrape or provide access to any television channels, films, live streams, playlists, guide data or subscriptions of any kind. MediaHelm Live ships empty. It contains no pre-loaded sources and no directory of sources, and it makes no third-party content available. All media and all sources are supplied, owned and controlled entirely by you, the user.

What MediaHelm Live actually does

MediaHelm Live is interoperability software. It takes a source that you already have — for example, your own tuner hardware, or a service you legitimately subscribe to and provide via a standard M3U playlist / XMLTV guide — and presents it to your own media server using the open HDHomeRun protocol. This is the same standard used by SiliconDust HDHomeRun hardware, Plex DVR, Channels DVR and Emby Live TV. Like those products, MediaHelm Live originates nothing; it is a format bridge between a source you supply and a player you own.

  • You are solely responsible for the legality of every source you connect and every item you play through MediaHelm Live.
  • You must not use MediaHelm Live to infringe copyright, to circumvent access controls or geographic restrictions, or to breach the terms of any third-party service.
  • You must not use MediaHelm Live to redistribute, resell, retransmit or make content available to anyone who is not authorised to receive it.

Prohibited use

The following are strictly prohibited and are grounds for immediate licence termination:

  • Using MediaHelm Live with pirated, infringing or otherwise unlawfully obtained sources.
  • Commercial retransmission of any content, or providing a "channel service" to third parties.
  • Any use that breaches applicable copyright, broadcasting or telecommunications law.

We may suspend or terminate a licence, without refund, for any use that breaches this policy or applicable law. MediaHelm Live is a licensed software tool; the licence does not grant any rights in, or access to, any content.
